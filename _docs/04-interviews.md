Andreea is a CS student, 4'th year, and a rather socially active person.
With a big group of friends, and experience in organizing and promoting events, she reprezents an ideal user for our application.

As a rather active person on social media, her inisghits provided in the interview can help us to better our product.

As we encourage to watch the full interview, we can mention here the key insights that we gathered:

- First of all, Andreea is using social media for private and also public activites that generate promotion 
- She finds out about events in an chaotic way, either randomly on social media, or friends decisions.
- It is rather hard to manage her group friends
- She enjoys both small and large events, and is eager to meet new people
- She would enjoy a better to organize go-outs with friends, an calendar is one proposed solution
- She missed events she would have went to if she knew about them
- Would add something to events that incorporates people feedback and sugestions
